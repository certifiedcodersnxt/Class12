## Key Concepts

1. **EM Waves**: Self-propagating oscillations of E and B fields
2. **Transverse Nature**: E, B, and propagation direction mutually perpendicular
3. **No Medium Required**: Can travel through vacuum
4. **Maxwell's Equations**: Predict existence and properties of EM waves
5. **Displacement Current**: Maxwell's addition, enables EM wave propagation
6. **Momentum**: EM waves carry momentum, exert radiation pressure
7. **Spectrum**: Continuous range from radio to gamma rays

---

## Maxwell's Equations (Conceptual)

1. **Gauss's Law**: Electric flux related to charge
2. **Gauss's Law for Magnetism**: No magnetic monopoles
3. **Faraday's Law**: Changing B produces E
4. **Ampere-Maxwell Law**: Current and changing E produce B

Together, these predict electromagnetic waves propagating at speed c.
